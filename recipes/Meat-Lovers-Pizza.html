Meat Lovers Pizza

Description

Homemade thin crust pizza, topped off with two types of cheese, bacon, ham pepperoni and hot sausage! A must make for meat lovers.

An American main course dinner with 4 servings that takes abut an hour and 10 minutes to prepare and 20 minutes to cook.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 C water
  • 1/2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/4 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1 1/4 C all purpose flour
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil if needed
  • 1/3-1/2 C Pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 C mozzarella
  • 3 Tbsp Parmesan cheese
  • 3-4 slices bacon cooked and chopped
  • 1/2 C ham roughly chopped
  • 1/2 C hot sausage cooked and crumbled
  • 1/4 C pepperoni slices
  • Ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough by combining warm water, heated to 115 degrees fahrenheit, with yeast and sugar. Stir to dissolve and allow to proof.
  2. In the bowl of a stand mixer, add flour and salt. With mixer running on low, slowly add yeast mixture. Increase speed, adding olive oil, if needed or desired. Mix until a ball forms, pulling away from the sides of the bowl. Transfer to a greased bowl. Cover and allow to rise in a warm location until doubled, roughly 1 hour.
  3. Meanwhile, cook bacon and sausage. Chop bacon and ham. Crumble sausage.
  4. Preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit.
  5. Grease a baking sheet with olive oil. Shape dough to desired size. Spoon pizza sauce over dough. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and 1/2 cup shredded whole milk mozzzarella. Add meat toppings. Finally sprinkle with remaining 1 cup mozzarella and ground black pepper.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until desired crispness is reached. Serve warm.
